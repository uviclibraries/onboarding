## Pre-Workshop Videos & Activities

To participate fully please do the following **before the workshop**:

- Go to the DSC Website and familiarize yourself with the information there: [https://lib.uvic.ca/dsc/](http://lib.uvic.ca/dsc).  You should know how to navigate around the site and find information about DSC tools and workshops.
    - Review and know how to find the [Workshops Schedule](https://libcal.uvic.ca/calendar/dsc?cid=7692&t=g&d=0000-00-00&cal=7692&inc=0)/){:target="_blank"}.

- Know how to find [library hours](https://www.uvic.ca/library/locations/index.php){:target="_blank"} and [floor maps](https://www.uvic.ca/library/use/floormaps/index.php){:target="_blank"} 
    - We recommend that you bookmark these links for quick reference.
